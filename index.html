Why Affirmations?

Affirmations are a big part of my daily life, every morning I wake up and try to recite at least 4 affirmations, 4 positive facts about my life. This might be difficult in the beginning however it is life-changing. You begin to look at life from a different perspective, you are grateful and realize what you have. I have learned to be grateful, being thankful for what I have. Step by step, day by day, one affirmation at a time.
